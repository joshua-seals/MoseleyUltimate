Origin Story

Moseley Ultimate Frisbee is a place for people of all skill levels to come, play, learn, and grow. The first game was played with 4 friends (dads) from Magnolia Green - Meridian and is growing quickly across the Magnolia Green community and beyond. We are open to all in the surrounding area so if you are interested in playing, learning how to play or just want to get a fun sprint workout in, come join us!
